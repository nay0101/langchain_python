Question: Tell me what is Brillar bank?
Answer: Brillar Bank is a financial institution dedicated to helping customers achieve financial success through various services, including savings, investments, and expert advice. They offer a range of deposit accounts such as Fixed Deposits, e-Fixed Deposits, and Foreign Currency Fixed Deposits, among others. The bank also provides online and mobile banking services to make managing finances more convenient for its customers.

Question: What is the difference between Fixed Deposit and eFixed Deposit?
Answer: The main differences between Fixed Deposit and eFixed Deposit at Brillar Bank are: 1. **Management and Access**: eFixed Deposit can be managed online with new placements and withdrawals made anytime via Brillar Online, whereas Fixed Deposit requires branch visits for withdrawals. 2. **Interest Rates**: The interest rates for Fixed Deposit start at 2.15% for a 1-month tenure, while eFixed Deposit starts at 1.85% for the same tenure. 3. **Minimum Deposit**: Both require a minimum deposit of RM5,000 for a 1-month placement, but for 2 months and above, the minimum is RM500 for both.
