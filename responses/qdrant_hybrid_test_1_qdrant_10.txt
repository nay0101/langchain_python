Question: Tell me what is Brillar bank?
Answer: Brillar Bank is a financial institution dedicated to helping customers achieve financial success through various savings, investment, and advisory services. They offer a range of fixed deposit accounts, including options for seniors, children, and foreign currency investments. The bank also provides online and mobile banking services for convenient account management.

Question: How many types of fixed deposit does Brillar Bank provide?
Answer: Brillar Bank provides six types of fixed deposits: Fixed Deposit, Junior Fixed Deposit, Flexi Fixed Deposit, Senior Savers Flexi Fixed Deposit, E-Fixed Deposit, and Foreign Currency Fixed Deposit.

Question: What is the difference between Fixed Deposit and eFixed Deposit?
Answer: The main differences between Fixed Deposit and eFixed Deposit at Brillar Bank are: 1. **Management and Access**: eFixed Deposit can be managed online via Brillar Online, allowing for new placements and withdrawals anytime, while Fixed Deposit requires branch visits for transactions. 2. **Minimum Deposit**: eFixed Deposit requires a minimum deposit of RM5,000 for a 1-month placement and RM500 for 2 months and above, whereas Fixed Deposit has the same minimum deposit requirements. 3. **Interest Rates**: The interest rates for Fixed Deposit range from 2.15% to 2.35% depending on the tenure, while eFixed Deposit rates range from 1.85% to 2.35%.
